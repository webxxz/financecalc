RD Formula

M = P × [ (1 + r/n)nt – 1 ] / (1 – (1 + r/n)–1)

Where:

  • M = Maturity Value
  • P = Monthly Installment
  • r = Annual Interest Rate (decimal)
  • n = Number of times interest is compounded per year (usually 4 for quarterly compounding in RD)
  • t = Time in years (duration in months / 12)
