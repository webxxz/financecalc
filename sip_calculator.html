SIP Formula

FV = P × [{(1 + r)n – 1} / r] × (1 + r)

Where:

  • FV = Future Value
  • P = Monthly Investment Amount
  • r = Monthly Rate (Annual Return / 1200)
  • n = Number of Months (Duration in Years × 12)
